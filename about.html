My story

During my career as graphic designer my focus was on creating corporate strategy & branding. I love the conceptual development of a unique corporate design for all applications.

User research methods are something that I follow with curiosity. Agile structures, meetups or hackathons - everything related to the digitization process pushes me forward. My empathy for users, who are not at all tech savvy motivates me to solve even the last problems via user interviews or user reports. I am thankful to involve this great thirst for knowledge into teamwork.

My personal mission is not only to create something beautiful. The design has only a sustainable effect, if it creates value for the environment. Download my resume
